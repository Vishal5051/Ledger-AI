# Economics

## Revenue Model
- **Free tier** – Unlimited audits with basic report (no PDF export, no lead capture).
- **Pro tier ($60 /mo)** – Includes PDF download, premium recommendation export, and priority support.
- **Enterprise custom** – Volume‑based pricing for >100 audits/month, with SLA and on‑prem deployment.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Paid ads & content**: $150 per acquired paid user (estimated 2 % conversion from blog traffic).
- **Referral program**: $30 per user (referral credit offsets CAC).
- **Partner integration**: $0 CAC – acquisition through Cursor/GitHub Copilot partner channels.

## Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Avg. paid user stays 12 months → LTV = $60 × 12 = **$720**.
- Enterprise contracts average 24 months → LTV ≈ $2,400.

## Funnel Math (Month 1 example)
| Funnel Stage | # Users | Conversion | Cost |
|--------------|--------|------------|------|
| Blog visitors | 5,000 | – | $0 |
| Free audit sign‑ups | 200 (4 %) | – | $0 |
| Paid conversion | 20 (10 %) | $60/mo | CAC $150 |
| Monthly revenue | 20 × $60 = $1,200 |

## Profitability Assumptions
- **Variable cost**: Cloud hosting (~$0.10/ audit). For 10k audits/day → $1,000/day ≈ $30k/mo.
- **Fixed cost**: Engineer salaries (~$6k/mo each), infra ($500/mo).
- Break‑even at ~5k paid subscriptions per month (≈ $300k revenue) covering $150k operating expense.

## Path to $1M ARR
1. Reach 500 paid users → $30k MRR.
2. Add enterprise contracts (5 × $2,400 = $12k MRR).
3. Scale to 1,700 paid users → $102k MRR (~$1.2M ARR) within 12 months.

All calculations reference real pricing (`$60 /mo`) and the audit engine's cost‑per‑audit estimate (≈ $0.10 per audit).
